## Applied Data Analysis

### 📊 [Rule of Law and Globalization in LATAM](https://mondhector.github.io/rule-of-law-globalization/)
An exploratory study on the relationship between economic globalization and the Rule of Law in Latin America (2019–2023).
* **Skills & Tools:** Python (Pandas), Quarto, Seaborn, Plotly, Virtual Environments. Handled time-series alignment for asynchronous institutional datasets and built interactive multivariate visualizations.
* **Key Finding:** Trade agreements like USMCA/T-MEC facilitate market globalization for Mexico despite regional variations in institutional quality.

### 📈 [Homicides & Vulnerable Populations Analysis (2024)](https://mondhector.github.io/homicidios2024/analisis_homicidios.html)
A spatial and time-series study on homicidal violence in Mexico, focusing on vulnerable demographics (Indigenous, Afro-Mexican, and migrant communities).
* **Skills & Tools:** R, Quarto, Tidyverse, `ggplot2`. INEGI survey microdata processing, temporal trend analysis, and geographic data visualization.
* **Key Finding:** Identified statistically significant upward trends in violence targeting Indigenous populations in southern Mexico over recent years.
